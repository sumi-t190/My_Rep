Spunhill Ltd was established in 1980 and founded on providing agronimic advice. The Business has developed to include animal health and general farm supplies, available at our stores in ellesmere, Mold and Haverfordwest.

The highly experienced team of basis and facts qualified agronomists work in the counties of Shropshire, Staffordshire, Cheshire, Herefordshire, Denbighshire, Flintshire, Gwynedd, Wrexham, Powys, Camarthenshire and Pembrokeshire. The Field agronimists offer a free field walking service and can provide a tailor made package specific to your cropping needs.

Spunhill are proud of their comprehensive annual trials programme in Wheat, Barley, Oilseed Rape and Maize. New varieties and agronomic inputs are trialled to test their performance under our local climate conditions to examine their suitability for our region. Open days are held at which visitors can see for themselves how different varieties are performing and have an opportunity to discuss their individual needs with technical experts.
